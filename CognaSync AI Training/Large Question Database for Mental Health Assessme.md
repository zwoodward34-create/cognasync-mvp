# Large Question Database for Mental Health Assessment via Speech

Below is a comprehensive database of **open-ended questions organized by category** that are optimized for speech-based mental health assessment. These questions encourage rich, verbose responses that generate sufficient speech data for prosodic, acoustic, linguistic, and semantic analysis.

## Category 1: General Emotional State \& Mood Assessment

| \# | Question | What It Elicits | Speech Analysis Features |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | **"How have you been feeling emotionally over the past few weeks?"** | Overall mood, emotional trajectory | Pause duration, speech rate, pitch variability [^3] |
| 2 | **"What emotions have been most prominent in your life lately?"** | Emotional granularity, affect quality | Vocabulary diversity, negative content, pitch [^3] |
| 3 | **"Can you describe what a typical day feels like for you right now?"** | Daily emotional experience, baseline affect | Speech rate, response latency, semantic coherence [^3] |
| 4 | **"How would you describe your overall mood compared to a few months ago?"** | Mood trajectory, change over time | Pitch variability, speech rate, comparative language [^3] |
| 5 | **"What's been on your mind recently regarding your mental health?"** | Current concerns, preoccupations | First-person pronouns, negative content, response latency [^3][^10] |
| 6 | **"How have your feelings been changing over time?"** | Emotional volatility, stability | Pitch variability, speech rate, temporal markers [^3] |
| 7 | **"What words would you use to describe how you've been feeling lately?"** | Emotional vocabulary, affect labeling | Vocabulary diversity, adjective use, emotional tone [^3] |
| 8 | **"Can you tell me about times when you've felt most happy or fulfilled recently?"** | Positive affect, life satisfaction | Speech rate, pitch variability, positive content [^3] |
| 9 | **"Can you tell me about times when you've felt most distressed or overwhelmed recently?"** | Negative affect, stress points | Jitter, shimmer, pause duration, speech rate [^3] |
| 10 | **"How do you recognize when your mental health is improving or declining?"** | Self-awareness, symptom recognition | Metacognition, semantic coherence, response latency [^3] |

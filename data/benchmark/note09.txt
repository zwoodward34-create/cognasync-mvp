Here's my sleep update. The Trazodone has been helping me fall asleep faster, which is great because I used to lie awake for an hour or more. I'm taking fifty milligrams about thirty minutes before bed. The one weird thing is I'm having really vivid, intense dreams now, almost every night. They're not nightmares exactly, just very vivid, and I wake up feeling like I didn't fully rest. I'm sleeping about seven hours total. Mood has been okay this week, no major stress. I did skip the Trazodone one night to see what would happen, and I was wide awake until two in the morning, so it's definitely doing something.

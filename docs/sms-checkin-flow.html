The 5-Number Reply Format
1 Mood 0 – 10 mood_score
→
2 Energy 0 – 10 ext.energy
→
3 Stress 0 – 10 stress_score
→
4 Sleep Quality 0 – 10 ext.sleep_quality
→
5 Sleep Hours 0 – 12 (decimals OK) sleep_hours

Mnemonic: M · E · S · Q · H  — Mood · Energy · Stress · Quality · Hours

Computed Score Coverage — SMS vs Full Check-In
Stability Score
Approximate
Dissociation defaults to 0. Flags dissociation_source='default' in extended_data.
Sleep Disruption
Partial
Hours only (+2 if <6hrs). Latency and awakenings not captured.
NS Load
Approximate
Stim load pulled from medication events. Caffeine not captured.
Crash Risk
Approximate
Nutrition omitted. Falls back to 50/50 SD+NSL weighting (already implemented in database.py).
Mood Distortion
Approximate
Derived from approximate stability. Directionally accurate.

✅ Approximate = computable, clinically useful, accuracy within ~15% of full check-in  |  ⚠️ Partial = computable from available subset, may miss edge cases

Recommended Send Schedule
Daily (Mon–Fri)
8:30 AM patient local time
5-number short
Core daily signal. Weekends off by default to reduce fatigue. Provider can override per patient.
Weekly (Friday)
Same 8:30 AM send
6-number (+ dissociation)
Adds dissociation field for full stability score. Optional one-word week reflection.
Onboarding
Day of enrollment
Long-form (2 segments)
Full numbered list with label descriptions. Sends once. Patients reference it when learning the format.
Ad-hoc
Provider-triggered
Short or magic link
Provider sends from patient hub. Short check-in or full React SPA link for pre-appointment depth.

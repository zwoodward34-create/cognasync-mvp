What CognaSync is

The bridge between what you track and the conversation in the room. The AI explains patterns. You and your clinician make the decisions.

It is not a therapist, a diagnosis, or a replacement for your care. It is a quiet layer that notices what changed, says it clearly, and hands it to the people who can act on it.

How it works

Three steps, one honest picture.

STEP01

You log the ordinary days

Mood, sleep, stress, medication timing, a short journal. A minute at a time, on your phone, in private. No streaks to keep, no judgment for an off day.

STEP02

CognaSync finds the patterns

Scores and trends are computed from your own numbers, not guessed at. When several signals point the same way, it surfaces them. When they disagree, it says so.

STEP03

You walk in prepared

A plain-language summary is ready before each appointment, for you to read and for your provider to scan. The visit starts further along than it used to.

Who it's for

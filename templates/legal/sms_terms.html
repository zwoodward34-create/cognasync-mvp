Program description

CognaSync sends care-related text messages to patients who opt in: appointment check-in links, recurring self check-in prompts, medication reminders, and voice-note invitations. These messages support your care between appointments. CognaSync does not send marketing or promotional text messages.

How to opt in

Enter your mobile number and check the SMS consent box in your CognaSync account settings. Consent is not a condition of using CognaSync.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies, up to approximately 3 messages per week, plus any medication reminders you have scheduled.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation message and then no further texts. Reply START to re-subscribe. You can also turn texts off any time in your account settings.

Help

Reply HELP to any message, or contact us at zwoodward@cognasync.com.

Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your information is handled according to our Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share your mobile information or your consent to receive text messages with third parties for their marketing purposes.
